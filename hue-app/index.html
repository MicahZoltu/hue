Hue Controller

Connect to your Hue Bridge

Enter the IP address of your Philips Hue Bridge. You can find it in the Hue mobile app under Settings → Hue Bridges, or in your router's DHCP table (Philips MACs start with 00:17:88).

After clicking Connect, press the round link button on the bridge within 30 seconds. The app will pick up the new username automatically.
